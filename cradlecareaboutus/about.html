WHAT IS CRADLECARE?

 Cradlecare is a medical record system that focuses on the easy and accurate handling of medical details of children. It provides services for children from the age of 0-17years old. The aim of Cradlecare is to reduce the rate of infant motality in sub-Saharan Africa by helping to document and manage the health records of their kids.

WHY CRADLECARE?

Cradlecare provides a notification and a chat system. It also provides a payroll system for hospitals. Cradlecare is the beginning of accuracy in recording medical details of children. Our aim is to reduce the rate of infant montality due to inaccurate recording of medical details and insufficient vaccinations and immunization.

HOW IS IT GONNA BE USED?

The Web application will be deployed in hospitals.And when there's a patient(child) the details of the patient will be inputted and be server.The patient then gets a reminder and alerts on the next appointment or vaccinations of the child .This saves time .Makes parents aware of their child's medical status and prevents misdiagnosis.
